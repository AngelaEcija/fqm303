Mª del Valle Zurita Lozano was born in Córdoba in 1994. Valle studied Biochemistry at University of Córdoba (2012-2016); in 2013, she joined the FQM-303 group as collaborator student. In 2015, she obtained an official research scholarship from Ministry of Education, Culture and Sport called “Beca de Colaboración” to develop a lateral flow biosensor to determine soy in milk. In 2016, she obtained another scholarship from University of Córdoba named “Beca de Iniciación a la Investigación” to develop an analytical method to determine allergen proteins in milk. In 2018, she finished her master’s in advanced chemistry at University of Córdoba.

Nowadays, she in done her PhD studies in Fine Chemistry and Nanochemistry. Her research is focused on extractive and (bio)sensor analytical platforms, where she uses nanomaterials, liquid chromatography with DAD and fluorometer, ultraviolet spectroscopy and fluorescence as separation and detection techniques.

For the diffusion of the science, she has contributed participating in different events of the University of Córdoba: Paseo por la Ciencia, Noche Europea de los Investigadores, Difusión de las Líneas de Investigación del Grupo or Jornadas de Introducción al Laboratorio.
