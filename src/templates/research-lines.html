Research Lines

  • Luminescence analysis: Lanthanide-sensitized luminescence, Long wavelength fluorophores, and Fluorescence polarization
  • Immunoassay, time resolved fluoroimmunoassay, long wavelength fluoroimmunoassay and immunoaffinity extraction and chromatography
  • Automated analytical methods: fast-kinetic methods, continuous flow analysis and micro and nanofluidic devices
  • Chromatographic and non-chromatographic techniques using pre- on- and post-column derivatization coupled with luminescence detection
  • Luminescence bioassay using nanostructures (liposomes and nanoparticles)
  • Separation and determination techniques
